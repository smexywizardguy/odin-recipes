Pizza Recipe

Description

Here we have a classic pizza recipe that will be sure to knock off your pants! Much like the Lasanga recipe this will be cheap and easy to make. You can add any toppings you might want and modify it just like a Honda Civic.

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salami

Steps

  1. Season and spread the tomato sauce over the pita bread
  2. Let the pita warm up in the oven for a few mimutes
  3. Apply cheese and toppings to your pita
  4. Wait a few minutes for the cheese to melt and the toppings to heat up
  5. Enjoy!
