Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is just a simple Lasagna recipe that will be sure to have you wanting more. The ingredients are easy to source and cook time is minimal. It will definitely become part of your weekly dinner rotation.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta by cooking in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare meat sauce by cooking sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Add to the pasta sauce for it to thicken.
  3. Stir cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
